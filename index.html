About Zukiswa

Empowering Communities Through Media & Creativity

Professional Summary

With over 22 years in the Media Industry, Zukiswa—affectionately known as Zukie or Sis Zukie—has been a powerful advocate for the Creative Arts, Media, and Community Empowerment.

Her journey, built on resilience and purpose, has empowered numerous brands and individuals, shaping their growth through impactful media and heartfelt storytelling.

As the founder of Ikhaya Lakho Media House and recipient of the Best Woman of the Year in Media 2024, she remains rooted in purpose—uplifting, healing, and guiding others into their destiny.

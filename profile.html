About Zukiswa

With over 22 years of service in the Media Industry, Zukiswa (also known as Zukie or Sis Zukie) is a passionate advocate for the Creative Arts, Media, and Community Empowerment sectors.

Her journey, forged through personal resilience and unwavering commitment, has been recognized by numerous brands through her impactful work. As Founder of Ikhaya Lakho Media House, she is dedicated to uplifting individuals and brands, fostering growth, healing, and connection through impactful media.

"Planted by God to plant men and women to their own destinies."

Professional Journey

Radio Career Beginnings

Started with stations like Khonza Nathi Igagasi Lobunye (now Vuma FM), Highway Radio Station, Imbokodo FM, and Itheku FM. Contributed to program development that helped secure Vuma FM's broadcasting license.

Media Leadership

Worked as Head of Business Development for the SO Network Group, encompassing SOTV Global, SOTV Africa, and SOFLIX Digital Media Hub, establishing and advancing each entity within the network.

Brand Management

Served as Brand Manager for Carly Sokhela (Professional Psychologist) and as Brand Manager and Head of Business Development for Albinism Arise founded by Sgwili Zuma.

Current Focus

Currently serves as a freelance radio presenter for Itheku FM and the visionary founder of Ikhaya Lakho Media House, pioneering both Ikhaya Lakho TV and The Love Talk Brands for Africa.
